Rich Dad Poor Dad
Robert T. Kiyosaki
4.1 (682,404) from Goodreads
Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki is a personal finance classic that contrasts the financial mindsets of two father figures—the "Poor Dad" who prioritizes traditional employment and financial security, and the "Rich Dad" who advocates for financial education, investing, and building wealth through assets. The book emphasizes the importance of financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and shifting from working for money to making money work for you.
The Psychology of Money
Morgan Housel
4.3 (268,146) from Goodreads
The Psychology of Money by Morgan Housel explores how human behavior and emotions influence financial decisions more than intelligence or technical knowledge. The book emphasizes that financial success is not just about knowing what to do, but about how you behave with money.
